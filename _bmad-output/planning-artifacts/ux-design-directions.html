Direction 1: Deep Freeze

Personality: Minimal, stark, professional. The darkest theme with near-black backgrounds and subtle ice blue accents. Panels are distinguished by slight shade differences rather than visible borders. This direction feels like a polished terminal — clean, focused, and uncluttered. Fire red appears only for destructive actions, maintaining a cool and controlled atmosphere. Best for users who prefer maximum focus and minimal visual noise.

Direction 2: Glacier

Personality: Cool and inviting with prominent ice blue accents. This direction uses a slightly lighter dark theme with a blue undertone. Selected channels, active states, and the guild selection indicator all feature visible ice blue, creating a more engaging and warmer feeling despite the "ice" theme. Borders are subtle but present, providing clearer visual separation. Active voice channels have a gentle ice blue glow. Best for users who want clear visual hierarchy and a distinctive brand presence.

Direction 3: Ember Glow

Personality: Bold, energetic, and warm. This direction inverts the fire/ice relationship by using fire orange as the primary accent for interactive elements, links, and hover states. Ice blue is reserved for informational and passive states like online status and unread indicators. The standard dark zinc background provides a neutral canvas that makes the orange accents pop with energy. Best for users who want a more vibrant, action-oriented interface that feels warm and inviting.

Direction 4: Dual Core

Personality: Balanced and distinctive. This direction prominently uses both fire and ice — ice blue for navigation and selection states, fire orange for action buttons and CTAs. Creates intentional visual tension between cool navigation areas and warm action elements. The sidebar leans ice, the message area stays neutral, and interactive elements lean fire. This is the most visually distinctive direction, clearly expressing the "Fire & Ice" brand duality. Best for users who want a bold, memorable interface that fully embraces both brand colors.
